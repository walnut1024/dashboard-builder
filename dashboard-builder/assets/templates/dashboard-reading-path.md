# Dashboard Reading Path

- Primary audience question:
- First scan: what the viewer should understand in 5 seconds.
- Second scan: what explains the first-scan state.
- Third scan or interaction: what details are available on demand.
- Always visible evidence:
- Hover-only details, if any:
- Filters or controls and the panels they affect:
- Caveats, source status, or freshness that must stay visible:
- Screenshot survival check: what remains understandable without interaction.
